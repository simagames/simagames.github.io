ZpellCatz "Seasons" Update (1.4.0)

February 7, 2025

Hey there, fellow Catz! Season 1 has started and with version 1.4 it brings a significantly better leveling experience in the Intermediate Realm.

Seasons

You can find everything about Seasons in ZpellCatz in the announcement from January. If you missed it, you can read it all here:

Seasons are coming to ZpellCatz

"Seasons" Update (1.4.0) February '25

*** New Features ***
- Added a seer skill tree (level 45+)
- Changed max level to 100, adjusted exp curve
- Added seasons with seasonal tasks, a season skill tree and a leaderboard

*** General ***
- Slightly adjusted the position of some dungeon entrances

*** Epic Items ***
- 'Bite of the Hydra': Added dark to fire conversion
- 'Storming Club': Now grants increased 'Shock Nova' range
- 'Light': Increased all res
- 'Mithril Garb': Increased defense
- 'Leather of Havoc': Increased pierce all

*** Intermediate Realm ***
- Map: Moved 2 uber bosses to earlier locations
- Adjusted uber boss clear rewards
- Changed required level for top tier gems from 55 to 80
- Reduced enemy bonus 'Pierce All' from 25% to 20%
- Slightly adjusted dungeon score calculation to match the new level cap

*** Text ***
- Renamed 'All Skills: +X' to 'Class and Path Skills: +X'

*** Balance ***
- Increased rare gem drop chance from 5% to 7%
- Dungeon level 45 to 47: Enemies deal 10% less damage
- Druid skill 'Healing Light': Increased HP gained
- Druid skill 'Photosynthesis': Added HP%
- Tranquility skill 'Adaption': Added defense%
- Enemies now gain 0.1% pierce all per enemy level
- Respec is now cheaper and available sooner

*** UI ***
- Software cursor now correctly scales with resolution
- Replaced flag images for language selection with a neutral icon

*** Bug Fixes ***
- 'Fist of Embers': Epic bonus is now grey for non-Fury
- 'Shadow End': Epic bonus no longer requires path Twilight
- NPC interaction markers are now correctly reset when leaving a room
- Fixed a bug where ingame skill hover didn't show skill info

*** Engine ***
- Game engine update
- Library updates
