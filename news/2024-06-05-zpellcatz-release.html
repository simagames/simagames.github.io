ZpellCatz Release 1.0.0

June 5, 2024

Hey there, fellow Catz. I'm super happy to announce that ZpellCatz is now available on Steam (https://store.steampowered.com/app/1447700/ZpellCatz/) and Itch.io (https://sima.itch.io/zpellcatz)!

Guides & Info

Probably the best idea is to play the game right now. But if you would like more information or are stuck somewhere, here are a few good sites:

  • A guide for new players can be found on Steam (https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3248995139) or on the SimaGames homepage (https://www.simagames.com/games/zpellcatz-assets/guide-for-new-players.html)
  • Don't know which epic items to craft / hunt for? Info on all epic items can be found here, sorted by tier: https://www.simagames.com/games/zpellcatz-assets/epics-t1.html, https://www.simagames.com/games/zpellcatz-assets/epics-t2.html, https://www.simagames.com/games/zpellcatz-assets/epics-t3.html
  • Want to chat with other players, give feedback or report a bug? Discord (https://discord.gg/D6CkrUu) and the Steam community (https://steamcommunity.com/app/1447700/discussions/) are good places for that.
  • Want to compare your dungeon score with others? The highscore for every dungeon (with a boss) is available here: https://www.simagames.com/games/zpellcatz-assets/highscore.html
  • You are a content creator / press member and your need further info or assets? Here's the presskit: https://www.simagames.com/games/zpellcatz-assets/presskit.html

Next Steps

The development of ZpellCatz was a long and exciting journey for me and I am very happy that there is now a feature complete version. I plan to release small patches and bug fixes every now and then over the next months. So, please let me know if you find any bugs or balance issues.

Finally, I would like to say thank you. ZpellCatz would never have been finished without the support of the community, Kickstarter backers, beta testers, friends and my wife and family. You are great!
:-)
