ZpellCatz season 5 is now live

March 2, 2026

Hey there, fellow Catz! Ready for a new season? Let's go!

Season 5: Gift of the Epics

Season 5 will be all about epic items. The following bonuses will be active during the next season:

  • Bosses drop an additional tier 1 epic item.
  • Greatly increased epic item drop chance (scales with dungeon level).
  • Epic items now have a randomized bonus power (scales with dungeon level, higher for lower tiers).

As in the last seasons, the overall difficulty is lower due to the strong bonuses. Have fun playing around with all kinds of epic items!
