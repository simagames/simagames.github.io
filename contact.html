Review Keys

As a game developer you get hundreds of emails with review key requests when a game release approaches. It's impossible to tell who's a real person and who's a scammer. So, please use one of these channels below for key requests:

  • Request via Steam Curator Connect
  • Direct message via Twitter to @LegendsOP (use the account that is listed in your YouTube or Twitch profile)

I won't be able to answer your key request otherwise. Sorry. :-)

Business

Are you a publisher or a fellow game dev and want to talk about business opportunities? An e-mail to legendsofpixelia [at] gmail (dot) com might be a good idea. Please start the e-mail subject with '[Business]' to let me know that you're a real person.
