Factsheet

Game
ZpellCatz

Status
In development, closed beta

Developer
SimaGames (Florian Siemer)
Solo developer from Europe (Germany)
Founded in 2014

Genre
Action RPG

Platforms
Steam Deck, Windows, Linux

Pitch

Zelda 1 meets Diablo 2 - with cats.

Description

Stranded on a cursed island, near death, you fight your way through dungeons full of loot to develop your cat hero's magical abilities. ZpellCatz is an action RPG with a dark campaign, exciting boss fights, hundreds of epic items, lots of magic and - of course - cats.

Timeline

  • Late 2018: Project start (in its current form)
  • Late 2021: Announcement with trailer, Steam page, etc
  • Late 2021: Prologue released
  • Mid 2023: Start for the private beta (full game)
  • Early to mid 2024: Planned release for the full game
