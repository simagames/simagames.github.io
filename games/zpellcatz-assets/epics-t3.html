Epic Items Tier 3

This page provides info about all of the tier 3 epic items available in ZpellCatz (game version 0.102.0).

There are currently 3 epic item tiers:

  • Tier 1 epic items commonly drop and most of them are craftable. Level range: 1 - 20
  • Tier 2 epic items drop from time to time and most of them are craftable. Level range: 20 - 44
  • Tier 3 epic items rarely drop and can't be crafted. Level range: 45

Footnotes

Some epic item stats grant bonuses to skills that affect a different skill for each hero class / path.
The respective skill is based on the position in the skill tree.
Have a look at this list:

  • [Hero A1]: Fire Ball (Mage), Ice Missiles (Shaman), Glaring Light (Druid)
  • [Hero A2]: Dark Wave (Mage), Lightning Totem (Shaman), Gaia Rush (Druid)
  • [Hero P1]: Meditation (Mage), Frost (Shaman), Radiance (Druid)
  • [Hero P2]: Arcane Intellect (Mage), Inner Storm (Shaman), Shield Mastery (Druid)
  • [Hero P3]: Shadow Grasp (Mage), Charged Soul (Shaman), Growth (Druid)
  • [Hero P4]: Explosion (Mage), Chill (Shaman), Photosynthesis (Druid)
  • [Hero P5]: Heat (Mage), Rune Power (Shaman), Healing Light (Druid)
  • [Hero P6]: Suffering (Mage), Frost Totem (Shaman), Sylphs' Gift (Druid)
  • [Path A1]: Shock Nova (Fury), Leaf Shield (Tranquility), Frailty (Twilight)
  • [Path P1]: Rage (Fury), Herbal Guard (Tranquility), Dark Harvest (Twilight)
  • [Path P2]: Haste (Fury), Spider's Bite (Tranquility), Refuge (Twilight)
  • [Path P3]: Thunder Claw (Fury), Adaption (Tranquility), Ambush (Twilight)
  • [Path P4]: Ring of Flames (Fury), Fortified Leaves (Tranquility), Fear (Twilight)
  • [Path P5]: Blaze (Fury), Winter (Tranquility), Inner Light (Twilight)
