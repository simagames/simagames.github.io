Mole Mine

Mole Mine is a vertical forced movement game. Can you help Mole the Mole to escape from the Mines of Doom?

Key features:

  • Navigate Mole through 16 procedurally generated levels
  • Discover a variety of different platform types
  • Find and spend gems to increase your chance of survival
  • Try to beat your highscore again and again in endless mode

But the best feature... It's free!
Play it in your browser here. (requires enabled JavaScript)

Controls:

  • Mobile: Touch left / right on the screen
  • PC: Arrow keys
