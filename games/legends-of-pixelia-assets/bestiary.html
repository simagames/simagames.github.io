Bestiary

THIS PAGE CONTAINS SPOILERS, PROCEED WITH CAUTION!

This bestiary provides info about the 80 basic enemy types found in Legends of Pixelia. Some enemies from earlier dungeons reappear in later dungeons in their normal form, but also in their game+ version (starting at dungeon 4).
This page does not cover information on the 42 boss fights. Bosses and boss adds have a different stat and skill setting compared to their base types.
